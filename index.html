About Kim

I help organizations communicate complex information with clarity, credibility, and warmth — so audiences understand, trust, and stay engaged.

I'm a professional voice actor based in Southern California, specializing in corporate narration, training, e‑learning, and explainer content. I work with organizations that need to communicate complex or technical information clearly — whether for onboarding, compliance, education, or internal communications. My delivery is articulate, calm, and approachable, helping audiences stay engaged and understand the message the first time.

Clients value working with me because I'm collaborative, easy to direct, and attentive to detail. I take direction well, deliver clean, consistent audio, and focus on providing exactly what the project needs — on time and without friction.
